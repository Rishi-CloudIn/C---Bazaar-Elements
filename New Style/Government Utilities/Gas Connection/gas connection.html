Gas Connection

LPG or Liquefied Petroleum Gas is one of the most versatile substances. From powering vehicles to being used as a cooking gas in homes as well as commercially, it has myriad uses. LPG is made using a mix of petroleum products such as butane and propane and is usually found in a gaseous state. It is stored in pressurized containers for most purposes.

Major LPG Cylinder Providers

Some of the major LPG providers in the country are mentioned below:

  1. Indane Gas :The Indane gas connection is available to everyone and even participates in various government schemes to provide subsidised gas connections to those who qualify for them. The registration for this connection can be done online and they even allow customers to book cylinders and refills over the internet, via the phone or using mobile applications. The connection is also transferable and can be transferred to different locations within a city or even outside it.
  2. Bharat Gas: This is another company that provides gas connections to domestic use in India. The company has even provided its customers with an online portal that can be used to apply for a new connection along with the traditional offline methods as well. To get a new connection, customers are expected to provide certain documents that determine if they are eligible for any subsidies or not. The connection is also transferable to any part of the country and is available to everyone.
  3. HP Gas: HP or Hindustan Petroleum, is one of the many companies in India that provides people with gas connections. The connections themselves are available to everyone in the country and are also eligible for subsidies provided by the government, should the applicant prove to be eligible for it. As with other gas connections, HP gas connections can be transferred from one place to another within a city and across the country as well.
  4. Super Gas: This is a private LPG company in India. The company is known to provide energy solutions that are efficient. The company is a leading LPG provider for home, commercial, and domestic LPGs. Individuals can apply for a Super Gas connection online. Proof of address and proof of identity must be provided in order to book a connection.
  5. Total Gas: These connections are popular in India as they can be purchased easily. The process to apply for a Total Gas connection is simple and can be completed online. The LPG division of the company is beneficial for the automotive sector and many households.
  6. Reliance Gas: They provide LPG services in cities such as Gujarat, Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, and Rajasthan. The company is known to provide healthy and safe energy to individuals. Products are offered to industries, hotels, and houses. Individuals can apply for a Reliance Gas LPG connection online.
  7. Jyothi Gas: Jyothi Gas Private Limited is known to provide excellent service all over Karnataka and has a customer base of over 2 lakh. The filling process is quick and individuals can book for a refill online. The company was founded in 1994.
Benefits of LPG over Other Fuels

Some of the main benefits of LPG over other fuels are mentioned below:

  • Scaling is avoided.
  • Corrosion effects are low.
  • LPGs can be used for several applications.
  • LPGs are environmentally friendly.
  • Maintenance is low when compared to other fuels.
  • LPGs can be stored easily.
  • Buring is clean.
